We know what we came for. It is not just about towers, cars and technology. Japan has been a cultural reference during centuries. The land of katanas and samurais, territory of the emperors and lately an amazing destination for any 'manga' (and anime) enthusiast. It is no easy to describe all wonders contained in Japan.

There is no reason to be afraid about the differences that give that particular personality to Japan. Our team of tour guides will conduct you to the most remarkable museums, high streets of Tokyo and temples, of course. Nihon travels also provides counseling from the UK for those who like travelling on their own.

We have been organizing trips from America to Tokyo since 1985, at our first office in San Francisco. After very succesful initial years we expanded our services to Canada and the UK.
